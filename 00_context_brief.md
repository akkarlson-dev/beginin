## The one-paragraph version

iN is a reflective AI practice built around one act: put something in before you decide what it means. The system holds it. Later, when you want to know what you've been thinking about, what keeps coming back, what your open loops are, you ask. begiN is the community layer: the space where people learn to use iN with increasing skill and confidence. Together they address one problem: 700 million people use AI, most use it as a search bar, and the gap is not access. It's knowing why and how to ask.
